[Hook]
I still feel your breath against my skin when the room goes cold
Every shadowed corner of this place has a tale we told
The scent of midnight on your hair is pulling me back in
I'm losing every battle that I swore I'd never begin

[Verse 1]
We left a trail of heavy sighs across this wooden floor
I hear your footsteps echoing right outside my door

I trace the lines upon my hands where yours used to go

[Pre-Chorus]
There's a heat inside my chest that I cannot ignore
Every memory of your lips just leaves me wanting more

I'm caught up in a storm of you and I can't figure out

[Chorus]
How your touch still haunts every single breath I take
I'm craving you in every little move I make
The shadows dance like you are right here by my side

[Verse 2]
I pour a glass of amber heaven just to numb the ache
